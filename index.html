About Hack Heist

GDG On Campus MIET presents Hack Heist – a thrilling 36-hour hackathon where tech enthusiasts, developers, and problem-solvers come together to innovate, collaborate, and build impactful solutions.

More than just a competition, Hack Heist is your platform to tackle real-world challenges, refine your skills, and network with like-minded innovators. With expert mentorship, interactive sessions, and hands-on development, this is your chance to shine!

Are you ready for the heist?

FAQs

What is Hack Heist? +
Hack Heist is a 36-hour hackathon organized by GDG OC MIET.
How can I register? +
Register through our website before the deadline.
Who can attend? +
Students, professionals, and tech enthusiasts of all levels.
How many members in a team? +
Minimum 1, maximum 4.
Will food be provided? +
Yes, for offline participants.
Selection criteria for ideas? +
Unique, innovative, structured ideas.
Are technical events online? +
No, it's an offline hackathon with extra events like CodeCombat.
Will internet be provided? +
Yes, WiFi will be available.
How to stay updated? +
Follow us on LinkedIn, Twitter, and Instagram.
